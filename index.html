Why Oriental Mindoro is my favorite place?

Oriental Mindoro is a favorite place for many due to its pristine beaches, untouched natural beauty, diverse marine life, rich cultural heritage, and relatively low tourist crowds, offering a serene escape with stunning landscapes, including lush mountains, pristine waters, and unique Mangyan indigenous communities; making it a perfect destination for those seeking a peaceful and adventurous getaway.

Oriental Mindoro

The inverted-J-shaped Oriental Mindoro boasts stunning landscapes, pristine beaches, and a lush interior with mountains, lakes, rivers, and rich wildlife. Best known for Puerto Galera, its world-famous coral reefs and dive sites attract divers of all levels. The province also offers mountain climbing, trekking, camping, butterfly watching, game fishing, and adventure trips into its wild interior.

History

Legend has it that long before the Spaniards discovered the Philippines, Mindoro was already among the islands that enchanted pilgrims from other countries. It was said that vast wealth was buried in the area, and mystic temples of gold and images of anitos bedecked the sacred grounds of this relatively unknown land. The Spaniards even named the island "Mina de Oro", believing it had large deposits of gold.
